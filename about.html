Our Story

Wayfare Wonders Travel is a premier travel agency based in Kigali, Rwanda. We specialize in personalized and seamless travel experiences that turn every journey into a luxurious and unforgettable adventure.

Founded by ISHIMWE Ange Aimee, our agency has grown from a small passion project to Rwanda's leading travel specialist. We believe that travel should be transformative, inspiring, and accessible to everyone.

Whether you're planning a safari, a business trip, or a romantic getaway, our dedicated team handles every detail — from custom itineraries and flight bookings to hotel reservations and visa support.

We're not just a travel agency. We're your travel partner — committed to making each journey smooth, stress-free, and truly exceptional.
